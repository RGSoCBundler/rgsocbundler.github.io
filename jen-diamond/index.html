I was working on an animation for a planetarium dome when I learned some Ruby so I could complete my idea. That's when I realized that programming was the missing link to my future. I started taking more programming classes and attended Railsbridge. I ended up inspired and started tutoring at Railsgirls, Railsbridge, RubyKids and started a study group. I have too many stories to tell and I am a resolute over-sharer. Buy me a coffee and you'll soon find out too much about me.

All summer we will be posting all of our #tools, #goals, #progress, and #achievements to this blog so others can learn from what we have learned!
